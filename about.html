About Our Organization

Working towards a Bodhmay Bharat based on equality, compassion and wisdom.

Who We Are

BS4 stands for Bouddh Samaj Sanrakshan Sanvardhan Sangh. Our organization works for the Bodhmay Bharat Mission, aiming to build a society guided by knowledge, compassion, and equality.

We follow the ideology of Baba Saheb Dr. B. R. Ambedkar and respect the teachings of the great saints born in India who preached humanity, brotherhood, and liberty.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a socially aware and enlightened society inspired by the teachings of Lord Buddha.

Through education, awareness programs, and community initiatives, we aim to promote peace, equality, and dignity for all people.

Buddhist Training Center

Our organization also runs a Buddhist training center where around 15 monks currently live and practice the teachings of Buddha.

They follow the path of wisdom, morality, and meditation while spreading the teachings of Buddha in society.

Our Core Values

  • Humanity and Compassion
  • Brotherhood and Social Harmony
  • Equality and Justice
  • Liberty and Dignity
  • Education and Awareness
