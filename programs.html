Our Programs

Working towards social transformation through education, awareness, and Buddhist teachings.

Buddhist Training Program

Our Buddhist training center provides spiritual education where monks live, study, and practice the teachings of Buddha. Currently around 15 monks are dedicated to spreading wisdom, morality, and compassion.

Community Awareness Programs

We organize awareness campaigns to promote social equality, education, and the values of humanity, brotherhood, and liberty inspired by the teachings of Buddha and Dr. B. R. Ambedkar.

Education Support

Our organization supports educational initiatives for underprivileged communities by providing learning opportunities, guidance, and awareness about the importance of education.

Buddhist Teachings & Dhamma Sessions

Regular Dhamma sessions, discussions, and meditation programs are conducted to help people understand the teachings of Buddha and follow a peaceful and mindful path in life.

Youth Empowerment

We encourage young people to become responsible citizens by spreading knowledge about social justice, equality, and the importance of community service.

Social Welfare Activities

We organize charity activities, community services, and outreach programs to support people in need and promote compassion in society.
